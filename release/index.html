Features

⚡

M-chip Accelerated

Metal GPU compute + Accelerate vDSP FFT. Process 20 RAW files in under 30 seconds.

🎯

Ensemble Focus Measure

Combines Laplacian + Tenengrad measures for best-in-class sharp-region detection.

🔧

Auto Alignment

Phase-correlation alignment corrects focus breathing up to 100 px between frames.

🖼️

Laplacian Pyramid Blend

Guided-filter weight maps produce seamless blend zones without halos or artifacts.

📷

RAW Support

Opens CR2, CR3, NEF, ARW, DNG, RW2, ORF plus TIFF, JPEG, PNG via LibRaw.

🔒

100% Offline

No internet required. All processing happens locally on your Mac. No data leaves your machine.

How to Install

1

Download & extract

Click the button above to download. Double-click the ZIP to extract HeyFoS.app.

2

Move to Applications

Drag HeyFoS.app into your /Applications folder.

3

Bypass Gatekeeper (first launch only)

HeyFoS is ad-hoc signed but not notarized. On first launch: Right-click → Open → Open

4

Stack your images

Drop a folder of RAW/TIFF files, choose your settings, and click Process Stack.
